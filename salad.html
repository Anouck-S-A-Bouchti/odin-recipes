How to make the most delicious salad

Elevate your dining experience with this vibrant salad bursting with freshness and flavor. Crisp mixed greens provide the perfect canvas for a medley of colorful vegetables, including ripe cherry tomatoes, crunchy cucumber slices, and thinly sliced red onions, adding a delightful crunch and a burst of garden-fresh goodness to every bite. Tossed in a zesty vinaigrette dressing made with extra virgin olive oil, tangy balsamic vinegar, and a hint of Dijon mustard, this salad offers a harmonious balance of savory and tangy notes that dance on your palate. Whether enjoyed as a refreshing starter or a light and healthy main course, this salad is a celebration of seasonal produce and culinary simplicity at its finest. Treat yourself to a symphony of flavors and textures with this delectable salad that's as beautiful as it is delicious..

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Chop the cherry tomatoes in half and slice the cucumber into thin rounds.
  3. Thinly slice the red onion.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and sliced red onion.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  7. Serve immediately as a refreshing starter or alongside your favorite main course.
