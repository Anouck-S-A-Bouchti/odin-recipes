How to make the most delicious Cheesecake

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this heavenly cheesecake recipe. Creamy and luscious, with a buttery graham cracker crust, this dessert is the epitome of indulgence. Each velvety bite melts in your mouth, leaving behind a tantalizing blend of rich cream cheese, delicate vanilla, and a hint of citrus zest. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a decadent treat, this cheesecake is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving more. Treat yourself to a slice of pure bliss with this irresistible homemade cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest (optional)
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup of granulated sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and 1 cup of granulated sugar until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract, lemon juice, and lemon zest (if using).
  5. Blend in sour cream until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  6. Pour the cheesecake filling over the prepared crust in the springform pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
  8. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the door closed for an additional 1 hour to prevent cracking.
  9. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack.
  10. Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.
  11. Once chilled, carefully remove the sides of the springform pan.
  12. Slice and serve the cheesecake chilled, optionally topped with whipped cream, fresh berries, or your favorite topping.
