How to make the most delicious Lasagna

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this sumptuous lasagna recipe. Layer upon layer of tender lasagna noodles, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and savoryItalian sausage come together to create a dish that's bursting with flavor and guaranteed to satisfy. The key to this lasagna's irresistible taste lies in the carefully crafted balance of ingredients and the slow simmering of the sauces, allowing the flavors to meld and intensify. Whether you're serving it up for a cozy family dinner or entertaining guests, this classic lasagna is sure to be a hit. Get ready to savor each delicious bite of this timeless Italian favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage (450g), removed from casings
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the Italian sausage in a large skillet over medium heat until browned. Break it into smaller pieces with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until the onion is softened and translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in all-purpose flour to form a paste (roux).
  5. Gradually pour in whole milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  6. Season the béchamel sauce with a pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove from heat.
  7. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  8. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
  9. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the baking dish. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles on top.
  10. Spoon a layer of béchamel sauce over the noodles, followed by a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  11. Repeat the layers of meat sauce, noodles, béchamel sauce, and cheese until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, then serve and enjoy!
